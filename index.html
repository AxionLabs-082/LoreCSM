LORE
ORIGIN:

Reze is a figure who appears with a warm smile, but keeping their identity as a weapon for survival. His past was shaped by manipulation, training, and lies that make it difficult to distinguish his true feelings and the role he had to play.

MOTIVATION:

Behind her relaxed and flirtatious attitude, Reze wants one simple thing: freedom. The freedom to choose, to love, and to live without being controlled by anyone.

DARK SIDE :

When the situation demands it, Reze is capable of becoming cold and deadly. He can use feelings as weapons, and emotional closeness is merely a tool to achieve his goals.

"Not all smiles come from happiness."
